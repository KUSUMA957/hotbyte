🏢 About Hotbyte

Who We Are

Hotbyte is a next-generation food delivery platform designed to connect users with their favorite restaurants quickly and efficiently. We aim to provide a seamless experience from restaurant discovery to doorstep delivery.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify food ordering by leveraging technology, ensuring faster delivery, better recommendations, and reliable service.

Careers

At Hotbyte, we are always looking for passionate individuals who want to solve real-world problems in food delivery and logistics. We offer opportunities in engineering, operations, and customer experience.

Our Team

Our team consists of developers, designers, and operations specialists working together to build scalable and user-friendly solutions.
